    About Me



    My name is Michael Anthony Carmelo. I was born into a military family in Ft. Campbell, Kentucky
    to two loving parents and along with my two sisters. I grew up with a huge fascination with electronics, music, and video games. This led to me starting my own guitar repair business as well as a market resaler at a young age. This passion for learning has followed me all the way into adulthood where I am a husband and father of three girls. I am excited to continue to learn and grow soaking up as much knowledge that I can so I can pass down this knowledge to my children.



    On this webpage you will find a plethora of projects that I have been involved in. It has sparse population as of yet but soon I will have each tab filled out with multiple projects along with some dabbling into data analysis.



    Thank you for checking out my page and taking the time to learn a little about me. Please check in regularly for updates.
